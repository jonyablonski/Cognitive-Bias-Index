---
title: "Blind Review"
description: "Removing identifying information to reduce bias tied to identity or status."
categories: ["behavioral design"]
tags: ["Fairness", "Hiring"]
how_to_use:
  - "Identify biasing signals (name, role, brand)."
  - "Remove them."
  - "Present neutral material."
  - "Evaluate using shared criteria."
  - "Reveal identity later if needed."
when_to_use: "When fairness and objectivity are critical."
tip: "Even small signals (like company names) can skew judgment"
use_cases: "Hiring, portfolio reviews, writing evaluation"
