---
title: "Disconfirming Evidence Prompt"
description: "A forcing function that pushes you to actively look for evidence that contradicts your current belief."
categories: ["critical thinking"]
tags: ["Evidence", "Analysis"]
how_to_use:
  - "Write your current belief or preferred option."
  - "Ask: 'What would prove this wrong?'"
  - "Search for opposing evidence."
  - "Identify assumptions behind your view."
  - "Test those assumptions."
  - "Update your position."
when_to_use: "When you feel confident or attached to a specific outcome."
tip: "Actively argue the opposite side as if you had to defend it"
use_cases: "Strategy decisions, product direction, personal judgments"
---
