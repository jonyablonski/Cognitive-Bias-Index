---
title: "Red Team / Blue Team"
description: "A structured debate where one side proposes and another critiques to surface weaknesses."
categories: ["critical thinking"]
tags: ["Debate", "Group"]
how_to_use:
  - "Assign one group to present the plan."
  - "Assign another to challenge it."
  - "Make critique safe and expected."
  - "Identify assumptions and risks."
  - "Revise the plan."
  - "Decide based on the improved version."
when_to_use: "For high-stakes or high-uncertainty decisions."
tip: "Rotate the challenger role to normalize constructive conflict"
use_cases: "Strategy, pricing, product direction, policy decisions"
---
