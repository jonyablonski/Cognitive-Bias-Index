---
title: "Decision Delay"
description: "A deliberate pause before making a decision to reduce impulsive bias."
categories: ["reflection"]
tags: ["Emotion", "Bias"]
how_to_use:
  - "Define what qualifies as a high-stakes decision."
  - "Set a delay rule (e.g., 24 hours)."
  - "Use the time to gather input or reflect."
  - "Revisit the decision."
  - "Compare initial vs. current thinking."
when_to_use: "When decisions feel urgent or emotionally charged."
tip: "Urgency is often a signal of bias, not importance"
use_cases: "Negotiations, financial decisions, sensitive conversations"
---
