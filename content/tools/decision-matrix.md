---
title: "Decision Matrix"
description: "A scoring framework that evaluates options against weighted criteria, reducing emotional or opinion-driven bias."
categories: ["decision making"]
tags: ["Scoring", "Comparison"]
how_to_use:
  - "List decision options."
  - "Define 5–7 key criteria."
  - "Assign weights based on importance."
  - "Score each option against criteria."
  - "Multiply scores by weights."
  - "Sum totals and compare."
  - "Sense-check the result."
when_to_use: "When choosing between multiple viable options."
tip: "If the result feels wrong, revisit your weights—not your instinct"
use_cases: "Vendor selection, hiring, prioritization, tool selection"
---
